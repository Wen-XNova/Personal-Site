About Me

I'm passionate about my personal growth, and often seek out opportunities to step outside my comfort zone. Whether I'm reading, listening to podcasts, watching movies or shows, or playing games, I'm always looking for new ways to expand my horizons. Fitness is a big part of my life, and I make it a point to hit the gym several days a week.

Professionally, I am drawn to the creative aspects of web and graphic design. I love using computers to bring my ideas to life, from digital art to intricate visual designs. The challenges of web design excite me, and I find great satisfaction in visualizing concepts and making them a reality.

My journey hasn't been without its challenges. Living with Asperger's has presented its own set of obstacles, but it has also shaped me into who I am today. I've had moments of courage where I've pushed past my limitations, resulting in rewarding experiences, including forming new friendships.

My Asperger's can be a strength. It allows me to focus intensely on tasks and gives me a keen eye for detail. I strive to create designs that are playful, dark, yet with a broad appeal. My ultimate goal is to secure a well-paying job as a web designer, which I hope will pave the way for a digital nomad lifestyle, allowing me to travel the world while doing what I love.

In terms of collaboration, I value respect above all in professional relationships. While I enjoy working solo, I also appreciate the support and help from others when needed. My ideal projects are those that challenge me creatively and offer the flexibility to explore new places.
